Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We specialize in web application development, including custom software solutions, e-commerce platforms, content management systems, API integration and Mpesa Integration .We also offer business and company registration services.

How do you ensure the quality of your projects?

We follow a comprehensive development process that includes requirements gathering, design, development, testing, and deployment. Our team adheres to industry best practices and uses the latest technologies to deliver high-quality, reliable, and scalable solutions.

What technologies do you use for development?

We work with a wide range of technologies, including but not limited to JavaScript, Python, PHP, WordPress and Django. We select the best technology stack based on the project requirements and goals.

How long does it take to develop a Web application?

The timeline for development varies depending on the complexity and scope of the project. A simple application might take a few weeks, while a more complex solution could take several months. We provide a detailed timeline after assessing your project needs.

How much does it cost to develop a web application?

The cost of development depends on various factors, including the complexity of the application, the features required, and the technology stack used. We provide a detailed quote after understanding your project requirements.

How do I get started with your services?

You can get started by contacting us via our website, phone or email. We will schedule an initial consultation to discuss your project requirements, after which we will provide a detailed proposal and quote.
